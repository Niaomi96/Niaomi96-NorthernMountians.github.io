Montis Lupus

  • Name: Mountain Wolf

  • Scientific Name: Montis Lupus

  • Origin of species: Unknown (Assume to be apart of Grey Wolves Conversion evolution)

  • Population amount: 800,000

  • Location: Somewhere in the Northern mountain range/hemisphere

  • Weight: 265 to 420 lbs (120 to 191 kg)

  • Height & body length: 4.5 to 8.6 ft (1.4 to 2 m)

  • Top Speed: 81 km/h (50.3 mph)

  • Gate Speed: 8 km/h (5 mph )

  • Diet: carrion, small animals, and sometimes supplement there diet with wild fruits.

  • Life expectancy: 15-30yrs

  • Heat cycle: 5-6 days.There is no breeding season for this species they go into heat during any time of the year.

  • Gestation period: 2 months or 65 days. Typically litter size: 1-4 pups per litter

  • Sexual maturity: Age 3

  • Rare occasions females can have up to extra pup. Pups aren't introduced to pack until 3 months of age, where they can keep up with the pack, as the pack roam there territory in search of prey. Pups are raised within the pack and learn the basics skills in order to survive in adulthood, they join pack hunts at the age of 1yrs of age following along from afar at the age of 2yrs they are joining pack hunts and making there firsts kills or tweaking skills they have learn. Females can have young in a nursery if, they have young around the same time.

  • Body dysmorphia between opposite sex, males are larger then females.
